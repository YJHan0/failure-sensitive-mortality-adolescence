# GBD 2023 Extraction Guide

Data source: IHME Global Burden of Disease Results Tool

Required selections:

Measure: YLLs  
Metric: Number  
Cause level: Level 3  

Ages:
- 5–9 years
- 10–14 years
- 15–19 years
- 20–24 years

Sex:
- Both sexes
- Male
- Female

Locations:
- Global
- High SDI
- High-middle SDI
- Middle SDI
- Low-middle SDI
- Low SDI

Years:
1990, 2000, 2010, 2019, 2023

Export format: CSV  
Include uncertainty intervals (Lower and Upper bounds).
